South Carlonia Gamecocks Football

The South Carlonia Gamecocks Football team is the football for the Universty of South Carolina. Their first season was 1892. Their first confernce was Inderpendent (1892-1921). However, now they are part of the SEC. They joined the SEC in 1990. Their first season in the SEC they were in 4th place with a 5-6 record. In 2010 the Gamecocks were invited to the Peach Bowl. However, the Gamecocks unfortunetly lost to the Florida State Seminoles 17-26. Recently the Gamecocks have improved, a 9-4 record in 2017 and 8-5 record in 2022. Desipte these efforts the 4 team playoff has prevented them from devolping a playoff berth. However, this year with the first year of the 12 team playoff the Gamecocks have a high chance of qualifying. Recently (December 4th, 2024) ranked 16 in the AP poll and big losses across the SEC improving their probablity making the playoffs. If they beat, the 12th ranked school in the country, Clemson they are likely to guarantee their spot in the playoffs.
